Massive Inferno Tears Through LA Residential Complex, Sparks Debate on Safety

A massive inferno tore through a residential complex in the heart of Los Angeles late last night, causing widespread panic and destruction. Initial reports suggest that the fire was caused by an electrical fault in one of the apartments, but the exact cause is yet to be determined. Hundreds of firefighters battled the blaze for hours, risking their lives to save the trapped residents. In a heroic display of bravery, the first responders managed to rescue everyone from the building before the flames could claim any lives. The incident has sparked a major debate about the safety of high-density housing in the city.
