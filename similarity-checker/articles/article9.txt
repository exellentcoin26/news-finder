Anger boils over in US cities as police killing of George Floyd sparks protests and riots

Anger boiled over in several US cities yesterday, as protesters clashed with police over the killing of a black man by officers in Minneapolis. George Floyd, 31, died on Monday after being pinned down by police, sparking outrage and calls for justice. The incident is the latest in a string of police killings of unarmed black men that have sparked protests and riots across the country.
