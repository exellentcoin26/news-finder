Fire Breaks Out in Downtown LA Residential Building, Displacing Dozens

In a dramatic turn of events, a fire broke out in a residential building in downtown Los Angeles last night, leaving dozens of residents displaced. According to eyewitnesses, the fire started on the second floor and quickly spread to other parts of the building. Firefighters arrived at the scene within minutes and were able to contain the blaze after several hours of intense effort. Fortunately, no casualties have been reported, but the affected residents are now being assisted by the Red Cross.
